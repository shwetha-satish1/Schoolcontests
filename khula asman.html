World of Khula Aasmaan

Khula Aasmaan (खुला आसमान) means “open sky”.

We have created this platform with passion to encourage creative expression by children, young adults and college students.

As the name suggests, it will not have any limits or boundaries. We urge you to explore the infinite journey, enrich yourself and benefit from it. The world of Khula Aasmaan is not only open and limitless, it is ever expanding as can be seen from our journey ever since we began in 2016.

International online art contest

We run a quarterly online art competition which receives entries from across the world. You can draw on any topic and submit drawings, paintings, cartoons, doodles, sketches etc.

  • 24 x 7 Online submission
  • Open for all age groups and nationalities
  • Artwork sizes : A5 / A4 / A3 / postcard
  • Use any medium of your choice
  • You can submit a maximum of 5 artworks
  • e-Certificate for all participants
  • Certificates for all shortlisted
  • Certificates for all honorable mentions
  • Gold, Silver and Bronze Medals
  • Dedicated web page for shortlisted artists
  • Opporunity to sell your artwork

Steps to download Certificate of Participation

  • Step 1 : Login as Existing Participant by visiting your dashboard
  • Step 2 : Enter your registered mail id & get OTP.
  • Step 3 : You will receive the OTP on your mail id. Enter the OTP & Validate it.
  • Step 4 : After validation, you can see the list of your entries on to the dashboard page.
  • Step 5 : At the last column you can see the option “My Certificate”.
  • Step 6 : Click on My certificate, you can see the list of contest names with their period & download option in which you had participated.
  • Step 7 : Download the pdf file of the certificate by clicking on the download link.
