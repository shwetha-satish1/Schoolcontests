    Who can participate?

    Contest is open to all school students in India age 13 and older. Prize of Rs 1000 will be awarded for overall first place winner. In addition prizes of Rs 250 will be awarded for winners from each of 7 categories.

    How do I submit my entry?

    It only takes a minute or two - then you're ready to submit your entry. Get started with Sign In with Facebook button and create your profile. Our writing contests are designed to encourage school students to participate in creative writing and win prizes.

    How do we choose the contest winners?

    Your content will be published and rated on our website by other users. By participating in this contest, you explicitely grant us a right to publish your entry (including any images, graphics and written content) on our website, our partner websites and our print publications.

    Contest starts on the 1st of every month and ends on the last day of the month. Last date to submit your entry for the current contest is November 30, 2022
